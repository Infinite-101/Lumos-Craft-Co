About Lumos Craft

Lumos Craft is committed to sustainability and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). We believe in creating products that not only bring joy and creativity but also protect our planet.

Our products, such as eco-friendly glue, sustainable candles, and recycled bookmarks,and oragami bookmarks are designed with both creativity and environmental responsibility in mind. Every step of our process, from sourcing materials to packaging, reflects our commitment to sustainability.
